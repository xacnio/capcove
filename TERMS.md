## 1. What Capcove Is

Capcove is a local screen and game recording tool: it captures a window, monitor, or a selected area to video, with an always-on Instant Replay buffer, a built-in clip editor, and optional YouTube live streaming. It runs entirely on your own device. It has no backend server, no user accounts, and no Capcove-operated cloud storage — see the [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/xacnio/capcove/blob/main/PRIVACY.md) for details on what data the app touches and where it goes.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Capcove can connect to external services at your direction, or contacts a small number of them automatically for core functionality:

- **Google Drive** — for backing up and syncing recordings to a Google Drive folder you control.
- **YouTube** — for live-streaming a recording session instead of saving it locally, and for uploading exported clips from the built-in editor, to a YouTube channel you connect.
- **Discord** — Game Detection fetches a public, read-only game-recognition catalog from Discord's API so Capcove can recognize games automatically; this doesn't require an account connection.
